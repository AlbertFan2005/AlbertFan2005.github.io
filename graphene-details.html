Synthesis & Experimental Methodology

Using Atmospheric Pressure Chemical Vapor Deposition (APCVD) to grow high-quality multilayer graphene. Our methodology ensures electromechanical stability through precise control of growth and transfer phases.

Methodology Insights:
  • System Control: Precise management of CH4 as carbon source with H2/Ar carrier gases.
  • Standardized Procedure: Ensuring repeatability through rigorous substrate cleaning and PMMA-assisted transfer.
