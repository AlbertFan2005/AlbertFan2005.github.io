Material Analysis & Technical Specifications

Instead of raw laboratory snapshots, the following key metrics define the quality and sensing potential of our synthesized multilayer graphene.

🔬 Optical Inspection

  • Coverage: Continuous film over 95% of target substrate.
  • Uniformity: Consistent multilayer stack confirmed via contrast analysis.

📊 Raman Characteristics

  • ID/IG Ratio: Minimal D-peak indicating low defect density.
  • Layer Count: I2D/IG ratio consistent with stable multilayer graphene.

Why these metrics matter: These material properties directly influence the input impedance and linearity of the analog readout circuit I am designing.

Cyclic Vibration & Reliability

The core contribution involves testing the electromechanical stability. By applying cyclic mechanical stress, we monitor the resistance fluctuations to ensure long-term sensor reliability.
