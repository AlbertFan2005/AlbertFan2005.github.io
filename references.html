Synthesis of Transfer-free Graphene Using Camphor

Core Focus: Developing an eco-friendly, one-step CVD process to seal micro-cavities for pressure sensing.

1. Methodology (Technical Recipe)

ParameterSpecification
Carbon Source2.5 mg Solid Camphor
Annealing1000°C for 100 Mins (H₂)
Growth950°C for 7 Mins (Ar:H₂ = 20:80)

2. Key Results

  • Monolayer Confirmation: Raman I2D/IG ratio of 2.3.
  • Structural Quality: Successfully sealed 3μm cavities with high yield (>90%).
  • Performance: Linear sensing response with time-constant < 500 ms.
