Analysis: Synthesis of Transfer-free Graphene

Source: Synthesis of Transfer-free Graphene Using Camphor for Air Pressure Sensing Applications

1. Methodology (Synthesis Recipe)

Focuses on the Copper Foil Wrapping method to achieve direct growth on $SiO_2$ without the need for a transfer medium.

ParameterTarget Specification
Carbon Source2.5 mg Solid Camphor
Pre-Annealing1000°C / 100 Mins (H₂ Flow)
Growth Condition950°C for 7 Mins (Ar:H₂ = 20:80)

2. Layer Identification (Raman Data)

  • Monolayer Verification: $I_{2D}/I_G$ ratio $\approx$ 2.3.
  • Quality: FWHM of 2D peak $\approx$ 34 cm⁻¹; Negligible D-peak.

3. Structural Results (Cavity Sealing)

  • Morphology: Successful 3μm cavity sealing confirmed by AFM.
  • Uniformity: Yield rate > 90% across the sensing array.

4. Device Performance

  • Linearity: Excellent linear resistance response to air pressure.
  • Timing: Response time < 500 ms; Detection limit < 0.1 Torr.
